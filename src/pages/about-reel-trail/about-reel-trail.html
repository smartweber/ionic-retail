About ReelTrail About Us
Love the outdoors? So do we!

ReelTrail is a community for outdoor enthusiasts to buy and sell outdoor gear. We believe in conservation and environmental awareness, so donating annually to trusted non-profits is a part of our mission. We hope to help recycle gear and keep it in circulation whether it's collecting dust or is sitting on idle in your closet, garage, trunk, etc. ReelTrail's platform is designed with low fees, and to keep all fellow outdoor enthusiasts protected from over-paying when reselling gear.

Our Story

ReelTrail was derived from a small storefront located in Charleston, South Carolina, where o utdoor enthusiasts + entrepreneurs + father and son Kirk and Ryan Leaphart sold outdoor gear through consignment. Dealing with sites like eBay and Craigslist were a huge pain, and the fees never ended. ReelTrail launched in 2017 in response to the frustration they experienced while trying to buy and sell outdoor gear on other online platforms. Kirk and Ryan knew there was a need for a market tailored to outdoorsmen/women. The solution then arose: An online community created and run by outdoor enthusiasts where buying and selling outdoor gear is simple and affordable.

 People & Our Planet over Profits

Relationships are the gold standard for us. We want to help others create small and large businesses through our platform. We know how it is when the big companies are over-charging, adding in hidden fees, and taking advantage of their users. Here at ReelTrail, we like to be transparent and simple. Oh, and inexpensive; ReelTrail's 7.5% compared to eBay's 12% and Amazon's 15%. We donate 3% of sales to different non-profits that are proven and trustworthy when it comes to helping our planet.
